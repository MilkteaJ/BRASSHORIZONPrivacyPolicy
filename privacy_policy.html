Flappy Plane Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2024

Overview

This privacy policy describes how we handle the data of Flappy Plane users. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy applies to all users of Flappy Plane.

Data Collection and Use

We do not currently collect, store, or share any personal information from users. Flappy Plane is a paid game, and no ads or data collection activities are involved during gameplay.

Third-Party Services

Currently, Flappy Plane does not use any third-party services to collect or analyze user data.

Future Plans

We plan to make Flappy Plane a free game in the future and may integrate ads. At that time, we will update this privacy policy to reflect the new data collection and usage practices. You can always visit this page to review the latest privacy policy.

Google Play Game Services

Flappy Plane may integrate with Google Play Game Services. These services may collect some basic user data, such as game progress and achievements, to provide a better user experience. The privacy policy for Google Play Game Services is provided and managed by Google, and you can view it on Google's privacy policy page.
